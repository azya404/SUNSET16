# SUNSET16

> *The DOLOS Management System wants what's best for you. Don't you want that too?*

**A psychological sci-fi narrative game by SuperNova Studios** — built in Unity, set aboard a decaying space station where compliance is mandatory and the truth is classified.

---

## Welcome Aboard, Technician

> Welcome Aboard, Technician Andromeda Vega.
>
> Your application to the DOLOS Corporation has been accepted. Your psychological profile is optimal, and your compliance metrics are exemplary. Your 5-year contract aboard the SUNSET16 begins effective immediately.
>
> Please note the following:
> Resist the urge to explore unauthorized areas of the ship.
> Disregard written log entries left by previous employees. They failed to adhere to company protocols, and their words should not be trusted.
> Take your supplement every day… without question.
>
> Your role is simple: work and comply. Albert will keep you company, and the DOLOS Management System will give you a sense of purpose. That's all you need when onboard the SUNSET16.
>
> Welcome to the DOLOS family, Technician. We are so glad you're here to stay.

### Commit Format

We follow a lightweight conventional commit style — keep messages short and prefix with the type:

```
feat: add new interactable to infirmary
fix: resolve ambient bleed between bedroom and hallway
fix(audio): ship theme now stops on non-whitelisted scenes
chore: update .gitignore to exclude render textures
art: update boiler room background sprites
```

| Prefix | Use for |
|---|---|
| `feat` | New feature or mechanic |
| `fix` | Bug fix |
| `chore` | Maintenance, tooling, config |
| `art` | Art or audio asset changes |
| `refactor` | Code restructure, no behaviour change |

Append `~yourname` to attribute specific commits when co-authoring on a shared branch.
